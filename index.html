ABOUT

Innovision, the annual departmental technical / co-curricular event of department of CSE, RCCIIT was inaugurated way back in the year 2013. Since then it has been a constant feature on the RCCIIT calendar every year. Each passing batch has passed on the baton to their juniors who have diligently carried forward the humongous task as to the organization of such an event without fail. Guided ably by the departmental head and teachers, each batch of students strive for only one goal, which is to surpass their seniors and present a bigger, better and more unique version of Innovision. Over time, Innovision has changed from a mere annual technical / co-curricular event to a coming together and a celebration of the whole department of CSE, students and teachers, juniors and seniors alike.

So with a pinch of nostalgia and quite a few handfuls of hope, we move forward to present Innovision 2020, and invite all our dear seniors,juniors and classmates from the department of CSE to participate and soak in the atmosphere of this year's Innovision.

CODATHON
RULES:

0. The contest will take place on HackerRank. Solutions will be judged on HackerRank's Online Judge. Therefore, every participant is required to possess a HackerRank account.

1. The contest consists of two rounds: Prelims and Finals. The Prelims will take place in two independent shifts, and participants will be allowed to appear for either shifts or both. However, only the best performance of a candidate will be considered for qualification for the finals.

2. Candidates will be ranked by their scores. If multiple candidates have the same score, then the ties will be resolved by the time taken to submit the solution to reach this score. The number of candidates qualifying for the finals will be decided shortly after the contest.

3. Definitive plagiarism, by any means, will result in immediate disqualification of the candidate in question. Plagiarism checks will be run after the contest.

4. Candidates must participate in all the phases of the contest using the same HackerRank account. In case of any any discrepancy, offending candidates will be disqualified.

5. Candidates are not allowed to look up solutions and strategies during the contest. However, it is allowed to look up language reference and documentation, if required.

6. The following languages are available for submission: C, C++, Java, Python, C#, JavaScript.

SHUTTERBUG

Shutterbugs 2020 calls out all the budding photographers to click the moment of life. It's a golden opportunity to show your poise talent with just a click of your camera. So innovasion 2020 brings you this platform to flaunt your talents in photography. So let us reveal what you have !!!! Before getting started with this Follow these rules and regulations to the letters.

Theme:

“Circle of Life/Parallel Realities”

Rules:

1.Pictures can be both Portrait and Landscape.

2.Basic edits are allowed (Exposure, Contrast, Saturation, Ambience). Black and White photos are allowed too. You can also use Double Exposure and Splitscreen.

3.You need to send both the original and the edited photos.

4.Watermarks are not allowed.

Details:

The qualifier round will be decided by a public choice likes based round on FB Page and then from the top 20 photos, the winners' will be Judges' decision.

Submission:

1.Each person can submit two photos at max.

2.The original photo is to be renamed as – Name_ClassRoll_Original and the edited as – Name_ClassRoll_Edited.
Eg :- (JagritSarkar_CSE2018/023_Original.jpg).

3.Photos are to be mailed at - cse.innovision2020@gmail.com

4.Send only JPEG/JPG files, not RAW.
