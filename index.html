ABOUT

Innovision, the annual departmental fest of CSE, RCCIIT was inaugurated way back in the year 2014. Since then it has been a constant feature on the RCCIIT calendar every year. Each passing batch has passed on the baton to their juniors who have diligently carried forward the humongous task as to the organization of such an event without fail. Guided ably by the departmental head and teachers, each batch of students strive for only one goal, which is to surpass their seniors and present a bigger, better and more unique version of Innovision. Over time, Innovision has changed from a mere annual fest to a coming together and a celebration of the whole department of CSE, students and teachers, juniors and seniors alike. So with a pinch of nostalgia and quite a few handfuls of hope, we move forward to present Innovision 2020, and invite all our dear seniors,juniors and classmates from the department of CSE to participate and soak in the atmosphere of this year's Innovision.

Theme:

“Circle of Life/Parallel Realities”

Rules:

1.Pictures can be both Portrait and Landscape.

2.Basic edits are allowed (Exposure, Contrast, Saturation, Ambience). Black and White photos are allowed too. You can also use Double Exposure and Splitscreen.

3.You need to send both the original and the edited photos.

4.Watermarks are not allowed.

Details:

The qualifier round will be decided by a public choice likes based round on FB Page and then from the top 20 photos, the winners' will be Judges' decision.

Submission:

1.Each person can submit two photos at max.

2.The original photo is to be renamed as – Name_ClassRoll_Original and the edited as – Name_ClassRoll_Edited. Eg :- (JagritSarkar_CSE2018/023_Original.jpg).

3.Photos are to be mailed at - cse.innovision2020@gmail.com

4.Send only JPEG/JPG files, not RAW.
