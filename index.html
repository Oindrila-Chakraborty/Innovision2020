ABOUT

Innovision, the annual departmental technical / co-curricular event of department of CSE, RCCIIT was inaugurated way back in the year 2013. Since then it has been a constant feature on the RCCIIT calendar every year. Each passing batch has passed on the baton to their juniors who have diligently carried forward the humongous task as to the organization of such an event without fail. Guided ably by the departmental head and teachers, each batch of students strive for only one goal, which is to surpass their seniors and present a bigger, better and more unique version of Innovision. Over time, Innovision has changed from a mere annual technical / co-curricular event to a coming together and a celebration of the whole department of CSE, students and teachers, juniors and seniors alike.

So with a pinch of nostalgia and quite a few handfuls of hope, we move forward to present Innovision 2020, and invite all our dear seniors,juniors and classmates from the department of CSE to participate and soak in the atmosphere of this year's Innovision.

GUIDELINES

TEAM FORMATION PROCESS:

1. Any number of teams or individuals from the CSE Department can participate.

2.No individual can be a part of more than one team.

3.The team size can vary from 1-5 people, from solo to five man team.

PARTICIPATION IN A PROBLEM:

1. Each team can participate in one to all the available challenges.

2. Each team can submit solutions for a maximum of 2 problems.

3. Each team has to provide justification regarding their understanding of the problem statement and give a brief description of the solution that they want to propose for the problem. The justification and brief description should not be more than 500 words. The team needs to upload the concept note and the detailed solution before the cut-off date and have to wait for the results of the first round evaluation.

4. Any software development tools, game engine, IDE, and/or programming language can be used for the event. If a team member uses a purchased tool licensed to him or her and the license is not transferable to other members, the member‚Äôs team must choose one available to all developer teams.

EVALUATION CRITERIA:

1. There would be two rounds of evaluation. In each round, the evaluation criteria will include novelty of the idea, complexity, clarity, feasibility, practicability and sustainability, scale of impact, user experience and potential for future work progression.

2. Your solution will be evaluated based on the following parameters:

Usefulness: The solution should have a positive impact.

Usability: The solution should be intuitive and easy to use.

Originality: The solution should provide an innovative or creative approach to an idea and should not be a copy of an already existing app/game.

Risk, Issues and Mitigation plan

SHORTLISTING OF ENTRIES:

First round evaluation: Jury will verify the concept note and detailed solution and evaluate your submission on basis of the parameters mentioned in evaluation criteria. Selection of participants for the final round will be done on the basis of the entries (concept note and detailed solution) submitted.

Second/Final round evaluation: Shortlisted participants from the first round will have to work on the prototype of their product within the mentioned timeline and will present the same to the juries. Selected participants will also get the format communicated through email from our team. Participants may please note that the maximum number of slides should not exceed 10 under any circumstances.

PROBLEM STATEMENTS
PROBLEM-1

(HEALTH) Currently, hospitals rely on mass marketing campaigns to inform patients of a relevant treatment or drug available to further improve their health. Build applications that can help hospitals, patients, pharmaceutical companies, and health R&D companies with more efficient and effective ways of communication by hospitals for better health outcomes and targeted communication to prime patient groups for a new treatment/drug leveraging the power of data analytics.

PROBLEM-2

(VEHICLE) In order to ensure pedestrian safety and vehicle speed limits in areas with high pedestrian density/residential areas, a system to detect the traffic density/ school areas/ residential areas and accordingly warn the driver about the speed limit for vehicles. The participating teams may use vision based systems which sense the speed limit signs or sign boards for schools/ hospitals/ accident prone areas etc. and may decide upon the safe speed value with which the vehicle needs to operate. The system needs to provide a warning to the driver, if the speed limits are breached. For the purpose of demonstration, the warning may be provided using a LCD or a seven segment simple display and a buzzer.

PROBLEM-3

(STARTUP MENTOR) There are many success stories around startups nowadays.Mentoring and guidance are very critical for early stage startups. Online platforms may help in reaching out to startups at the grass root level including college students. So design a software solution for,

a)Creating startups and mentors that can help startups with market trends, etc.

b)Helping startups to reach mentors in required domains.

c)Helping startups to reach out to colleges for talent based on different criterias.

PROBLEM-4

(SPACE MANAGEMENT) Efficient office space management is an essential requirement for the smooth functioning of any business. As the workforce and scale of a company grows, it becomes harder to manage the available space. This space not only shelters all employees, but is a strategic tool for productivity, collaboration, and growth. The right office environment can equip employees with the required motivation to tackle big, important projects. You are expected to develop apps/software that allows use of available space effectively.

PROBLEM-5

(FinTech) The finance industry has already experienced many changes and disruptions caused by FinTechs. Technologies such as mobile banking, mobile payment, and biometrics have changed how many consumers interact with their banks and financial institutions every day. You are expected to build innovative solutions and products for the hackathon that are applicable to the Fintech Industry.

PROBLEM-6

(BLOCKCHAIN) Blockchain as a technology is likely to disrupt multiple industries and make business operations more transparent, efficient & secure. We are at an exciting phase in the evolution of Blockchain-based solutions, as it provides new infrastructure. Your submission should clearly explain how your solution leverages blockchain principles and why blockchain is the core component of your solution.

PROBLEM-7

(CYBER BULLYING) Cyber bullying involves posting and sharing wrong, private, negative, harmful information about victims. We are looking for the software solution to curb such bullying/harassment in cyberspace. Such a solution is expected to,

a) Work on social media such as twitter, facebook,etc. and on SMS, etc.

b) Should alert the authorities.

c) Facility to report such incidents to authority.

Any additional useful features are welcome.

PROBLEM-8

(CAPTCHA) Visually impaired citizens face a lot of problems with the CAPTCHA authentication process. In some of the applications, OTP is given as an alternative but the feature is absent in most apps. In some of the apps, audio captcha is provided but many citizens find it difficult to decipher. You are expected to design an effective solution for CAPTCHA or develop an alternative that can address the problems faced by the visually impaired citizens.

PROBLEM-9

(FAKE NEWS) Fake news has been a hot topic in the last few years in the form of Troll Farms and these Hoax News attempt to create public unrest like Lynching, Cyber Mobbing, Subvert and influence the public perceptions using social media platforms. You are expected to develop a solution that will detect Fake news like Offensive Text (Comment, Post, Feeds, etc.), Offensive Images (Original or Morphed Pictures) and Offensive Multimedia Videos (Original or Fake Videos) across the Social Media websites using keywords crawling, APIs, Reverse Image and AI/ML/Data Mining techniques and original source of posting and nearer/proximate profiles.

PROBLEM-10

(INNOVATION) Innovation: Designing a new and innovative software or a hardware product can be a challenging process filled with uncertainties. From a business perspective, you need to explore and keep at the forefront of what is happening in the market, search for unique opportunities until you discover a meaningful solution. You are not limited to the above nine problems, you can submit any innovative solution that you think is relevant in today‚Äôs society.
